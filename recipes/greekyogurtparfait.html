Khubz with Hummus



Description

A Greek Yogurt Parfait is a simple and nutritious breakfast made by layering creamy Greek yogurt with fresh fruit and crunchy granola. Rich in protein and naturally sweetened with fruit and honey, it is a refreshing, no-cook meal that can be prepared in just a few minutes. Perfect for busy mornings, this parfait offers a balance of flavor, texture, and nutrition.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • ½ cup granola
  • 1 cup mixed fresh fruit (such as strawberries, blueberries, bananas, or mangoes)
  • 1 tablespoon honey (optional)
  • A few nuts or seeds for topping (optional)

Steps

  1. Wash and prepare the fruit by slicing or dicing it into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Spoon a layer of Greek yogurt into a glass or bowl.
  3. Add a layer of granola over the yogurt.
  4. Add a layer of fresh fruit.
  5. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used.
  6. Drizzle honey on top, if desired.
  7. Garnish with nuts or seeds for extra crunch.
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy.
Prep Time: 10 minutes
Cook Time: 0 minutes
Servings: 1-2
